# Other Integrations

These projects are maintained by third parties. You need a working Beszel instance accessible from your device to use them.

## TRMNL e-ink dashboard

**GitHub:** [mr-karan/trmnl-beszel](https://github.com/mr-karan/trmnl-beszel)

A [TRMNL](https://usetrmnl.com) e-ink plugin that shows your Beszel server fleet at a glance — CPU, memory, disk, uptime, load average, and temperature for every host you monitor.
